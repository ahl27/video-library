Module: Single-Cell Analysis

This module will guide you through single-cell transcriptomics analysis in Galaxy.

Below you will find all the tutorials for this module, everything you need to follow these trainings is listed here.

Single-cell RNA-seq analysis is a rapidly evolving field at the forefront of transcriptomic research, used in high-throughput developmental studies

An introduction to scRNA-seq data analysis (Lecture)

Description: Single-cell RNA-seq analysis is a rapidly evolving field at the forefront of transcriptomic research, used in high-throughput developmental studies and rare transcript studies to examine cell heterogeneity within a populations of cells. The cellular resolution and genome wide scope make it possible to draw new conclusions that are not otherwise possible with bulk RNA-seq. Slides created by Mehmet Tekman.
Length: 20 minutes
Captions: Mehmet Tekman
Created: 15 February 2021
